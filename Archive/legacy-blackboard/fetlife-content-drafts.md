**FetLife Post Draft:**

Every year, we lose community members to solo play accidents. Self-bondage failures. Medical emergencies during intense scenes. Equipment malfunctions. None of these happen to reckless people — they happen to experienced kinksters who just got unlucky once.

**This guide could literally save your life:**
• Why every solo player needs a safety timer system
• How to set up emergency contacts who don't need to know your kinks
• The complete solo play safety checklist 
• What to do before, during, and after your scenes
• Real talk about the "it won't happen to me" fallacy

Solo play can be incredibly fulfilling, but it comes with unique risks. A safety timer isn't about being paranoid — it's about being responsible. It's the same logic as wearing a seatbelt.
