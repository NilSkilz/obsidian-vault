# Competitor Intelligence Report - March 2026

**Research Date:** March 20, 2026  
**Analyst:** Subagent Research Team  
**Focus:** Current state of Tethered's main competitors and new market entrants  

## Executive Summary

**Key Takeaway:** No direct competitors have emerged in Tethered's core "safety-first for new encounters" positioning. Both main competitors (Obedience & BeMoreKinky) are doubling down on their established strengths - habit tracking and discovery/data insights respectively. This represents a significant **blue ocean opportunity** for Tethered to own the safety niche.

## 5. Strategic Recommendations

### Immediate Opportunities (Q2 2026)
1. **Content leadership:** Create safety-focused blog content to counter competitors' thought leadership
2. **Pricing positioning:** Target $45-50/year (between Obedience $30 and BeMoreKinky $35)

### Differentiation Strategy
**Tethered's Unique Value Prop:** "The only app focused on keeping you safe when meeting new partners"

**Key Messaging:**
- Obedience = habit tracking for established couples  
- BeMoreKinky = discovery for long-term partners
- Tethered = safety for everyone, especially new encounters

### Competitive Response Plan
- **Don't chase their strengths:** Let Obedience own habit tracking, let BeMoreKinky own data insights
- **Own the safety narrative:** Position as essential for community safety
- **Target neglected audiences:** New practitioners, munch organizers, community leaders

## Key Takeaway for Rob

**The good news:** No one is competing directly with Tethered's safety-first positioning. Both main competitors are deepening their existing market positions rather than expanding into safety.

**The challenge:** Obedience and BeMoreKinky are becoming increasingly sophisticated in content marketing and thought leadership. If Tethered wants to compete for mindshare, it needs to establish authority in the safety domain.

**The opportunity:** A clear path exists to own the "meeting new people safely" narrative - from first munch attendance to scene negotiation with new partners. This is a underserved, high-value market that neither competitor is addressing.

**Next steps:** Consider launching a safety-focused content series to establish Tethered as the go-to resource for kink safety, particularly for newcomers and event organizers.
